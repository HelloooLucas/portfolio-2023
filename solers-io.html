Solers.io

Solers.io is a job board for developers
that takes a twist at skills assertion.
Once a developer has declared their skills,
they can pass online certifications
that would act as proof for recruiters
and remove the need for further technical testing

The website is fully responsive,
allows developers to create an account,
declare skills, pass certifications
and apply to job offers.
I did only the frontend part of the project

Certifying your skills
once and for all

As soon as you validate a skill,
it becomes “validated” for job offers.
Validate all required skills and you’re ready to apply!
